Privacy Policy for Browser Cleaner

Effective Date: 18 January 2026

Browser Cleaner (“we”, “our”, or “the Extension”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Information We Collect

The extension is designed to protect your privacy. We do not collect, transmit, or share personal data with any third parties. The extension may access the following browser data only when you explicitly request it:

  • Browsing data (history, cache, cookies, site data) for cleanup purposes.
  • Tabs to provide tab-specific cleaning options.
  • User preferences (which data types to clean, schedules) stored locally in your browser.

2. How We Use Your Data

Data accessed by the extension is used solely to provide its cleaning functionality, including:

  • Clearing cookies, cache, and history.
  • Removing site-specific data.
  • Applying user-defined preferences and schedules.

All operations are performed locally in your browser.

3. Permissions

The extension requires certain permissions to function correctly:

  • Browsing Data & History: To clear browsing data on user request.
  • Cookies: To remove cookies and enhance privacy.
  • Tabs & Host Permissions: To clean data on specific websites.
  • Storage: To save user preferences locally.
  • Remote Code (if applicable): To update extension scripts securely for bug fixes or improvements.

4. Data Sharing and Security

We do not share, sell, or transmit any personal information. All user data remains on your device unless you choose to share it manually.

5. User Rights

You can:

  • Clear your browsing data using the extension at any time.
  • Delete your stored preferences from the extension.
  • Remove the extension entirely from your browser.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will be posted here with the effective date. Your continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of any changes.
